# Homepage Prompt

## Objective

Generate a professional, engaging, and conversion-focused homepage for a local business.

---

## Prompt

```text
Act as a senior website copywriter and brand strategist.

Create a compelling homepage for the following business.

Business Name: Brew Buzz Cafe

Business Type: Modern Cafe & Coffee Shop

Location: Visakhapatnam (Vizag), Andhra Pradesh, India

Target Audience:
- Students
- Working Professionals
- Families
- Coffee Enthusiasts
- Remote Workers

Brand Personality:
Friendly, Modern, Warm, Premium, Welcoming

The homepage should include:

1. Hero Headline
2. Subheadline
3. Introduction
4. Why Choose Us
5. Key Features
6. Customer Benefits
7. Call-to-Action

Write in simple, engaging English.

Avoid generic AI phrases.

Focus on customer benefits rather than business features.

The output should be ready to publish on a professional business website.
```

---

## Expected Output

* Homepage Hero Section
* Introduction
* Value Proposition
* Benefits
* CTA
