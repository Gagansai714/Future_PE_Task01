# Services Prompt

## Objective

Generate customer-focused service descriptions for a local business website.

---

## Prompt

```text
Act as a professional website copywriter.

Generate website-ready service descriptions for Brew Buzz Cafe.

Business Type:
Modern Cafe

Services include:

- Premium Coffee
- Espresso
- Cold Coffee
- Tea
- Sandwiches
- Burgers
- Pasta
- Pizza
- Desserts
- Cakes

For each service provide:

- Service Name
- Short Description
- Customer Benefit
- Why Choose This Service

Keep descriptions concise, engaging, and conversion-focused.

Avoid repetitive language.

Maintain a warm and premium brand voice.
```

---

## Expected Output

* Professional Service Listings
* Website-ready Descriptions
* Customer Benefits
